Good to know

Questions, answered.

Shipping, returns, care, and how commissions work.

How long does a commission take?
It depends on the medium — pencil sketches take 5–7 days, paintings 10–14. You'll always get a timeline with your quote before we begin.
Do you ship across India? Internationally?
Yes — complimentary insured shipping across India, dispatched in 3–5 business days for ready-made pieces. International shipping is coming soon.
How are fragile pieces packed?
Clay, cement, mirror and glass pieces are hand-packed in protective, recyclable materials and shipped insured, so they arrive exactly as they left the studio.
What is your return policy?
Ready-made shop pieces can be returned within 7 days if unused and undamaged. Bespoke commissions are made to order and can't be returned, which is why we approve every detail with you before starting.
How do I care for my piece?
Most pieces need only a gentle wipe with a dry, soft cloth. Avoid moisture and direct sunlight. Specific care notes come with each order.
How do payments work?
Shop pieces are paid at checkout. For commissions, you approve the approach and quote first — no payment until you're happy with the plan.
Can I order in bulk or for an event?
Absolutely. We take corporate and gifting programmes with dedicated timelines — reach out via the contact page.
